With over 26 years of experience building and managing libraries from the ground up, I bring a passion for creating spaces where knowledge is easy to find and a joy to explore. From The Royal Colosseum University to Sceptre College, Beaconhouse School, British Overseas School, and Aisha Bawany Girls’ Secondary School, I have transformed empty rooms into vibrant, well-organized libraries aligned with HEC standards.

Skilled in library management systems including LIMS, PRIMASOFT, BEAMS, KOHA, and Web Dewey, I combine technical expertise with a personal touch—whether guiding a student to the perfect resource or curating collections that inspire. I believe a library should feel welcoming, organized, and alive, and I take pride in making that happen every day.
